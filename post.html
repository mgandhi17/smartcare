Smart Care

Automated Customer Service Solution

By Mohit Gandhi on April 28, 2016
59 % of customers will abandon a purchase if they cannot find quick answers to their questions

Smart Care is a channel agnostic platform that enables brands to foster customer service by automatically responding to customer queries based on text analysis using natural language processing techniques.

On an average companies take 22.9 hours to respond, while customers expect a response within 6 hours.

This platform will bridge the gap between customer expectations and cost of delivering service by detecting and responding to customer questions without human intervention.

How does the Platform work ?

Incoming communications across all channels including emails, communities, chats, web forms are routed to the smart care platform. Here, the raw text is analyzed and customer issue is identified with the help of a classifier. At the heart of this platform is a convolutional neural network which powers all the analysis. A key feature of the system is that it continuously learns and improves its performance.
